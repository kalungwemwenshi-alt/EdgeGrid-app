About EdgeGrid Traders Capital

Your partner in financial freedom, based right here in Livingstone, Zambia.

Redefining Trading Education in Zambia

Our journey began in 2019 as Topnotch Traders Capital. We noticed a significant gap in the market for quality, transparent, and results-driven forex trading education in Zambia.

In 2023, after several strategic partners came onboard to expand our reach and resources, we officially rebranded to EdgeGrid Traders Capital. Today, we continue to bridge that gap with more power and precision than ever before.

Our core philosophy remains simple: we earn when you earn. With our 'Learn Now, Pay Later' model, we absorb the upfront risk of your education so that you can focus entirely on mastering the markets. Only when you are consistently profitable do you pay us back.

Why Trust Us?

  • Proven track record of profitable students
  • Zero hidden fees in our Learn Now Pay Later model
  • Direct access to institutional-level mentors
